ABOUT THE CEO

Mr. Jonathan M. Maximo

Founder & CEO — APGREYD Marketing Services

Mr. Jonathan M. Maximo started his journey in the media and entertainment industry as a former staff member of ABS-CBN from 2012–2015, serving as a Coordinator and Researcher for It's Showtime. During his stay in the network, he handled several well-known segments including Kalookalike, Bida Kapamilya, Tandang Pananong, Pogay, That's My Tomboy, Jambunganga, and Mini Me.

After his ABS-CBN career, he expanded his experience in entertainment and business by becoming a Co-Manager of G2B under Viva Entertainment. He also worked as a Marketing Officer for various food and lifestyle brands, helping businesses improve their visibility and customer engagement.

He later became a Business Development Officer for Fill in the Blank Inc., handling projects and events at the World Trade Center Philippines, where he strengthened his expertise in partnerships, brand activations, and corporate collaborations.

As digital platforms rapidly evolved, Jonathan transitioned into the online gaming and digital marketing industry, becoming a Digital Marketing Manager and B2B specialist for BingoPlus and several online casino brands. He handled operations, marketing strategies, team management, player acquisition systems, and business partnerships that contributed to company growth and engagement.

He also served as Business Development Manager for UTOL and RSM Entertainment, focusing on strategic partnerships, media collaborations, events, and large-scale marketing campaigns.

Today, Jonathan M. Maximo is the Founder and CEO of APGREYD Marketing Services, a growing agency focused on digital marketing, influencer campaigns, partnership marketing, content production, and business growth solutions. Through APGREYD, his vision is to help brands scale through innovative ideas, strategic collaborations, and results-driven marketing systems.
